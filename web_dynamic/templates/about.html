About Us

Cycling is a great way to stay fit and healthy. It is also a fun way to explore new places. At Rent-a-bike, we are passionate about cycling. We believe that everyone should have access to a bike. That is why we offer a wide range of bikes for rent. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced cyclist, we have a bike for you. Our bikes are well maintained and are available at affordable prices. We also have a team of experienced mechanics who ensure that our bikes are in good condition.

Rent-a-bike is the best place to rent a bike in Nairobi. Our aim is to provide our customers with the best cycling experience. We are committed to providing high-quality bikes and excellent customer service. We are always looking for ways to improve our service and we welcome your feedback. If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to contact us. We look forward to hearing from you.

Our Origin

A popular weekend activity when I was in campus was cycling where you could provide you ID and pay up then get a bicycle for the entire day. After finishing campus and moving away from the school for work I realised how difficult it was to find a one-stop shop where you could rent any bike that was both efficient and reliable. In most cases the best shot was to go directly to the shop which was an option due to the added hustle of transporting the bike to where I lived.


As such, I decided to come up with a platform through which you could easily rent a bike and have it delivered be it to your home, office, school or just below your hiking trail.

I hope that the platform encourages more people to choose cycling which is both good for your health and for the environment. Cycling is flexible and cheap compared to alternative transport means.



Though this is a passion project, it is also a project for the program run by Holberton School.
