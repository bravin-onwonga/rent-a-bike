Our bikes are well maintained and are available at affordable prices. We also have a team of experienced mechanics who ensure that our bikes are in good condition. Rent-a-bike is the best place to rent a bike in Nairobi.

Getting Started

We have provided a simple and seamless way for your to rent a bike. Simply click on the rent button to rent the bike you like. To view your selected bikes click on the cart icon, click on the x icon to remove the item from cart. If you want more information click anywhere but the rent button on the image card. You will be required to provide self-identifying information i.e. your official names and your ID or Passport number and once your information has been verified your bike(s) will be delivered to your location as indicated.


Once your identity is verified you will have an account created and you will just login and begin the return process. Alternatively, you could drop them off to any of our lessors and we will pick them up.

Our Origin

A popular weekend activity when I was in campus was cycling where you could provide you ID and pay up then get a bicycle for the entire day. After finishing campus and moving away from the school for work I realised how difficult it was to find a one-stop shop where you could rent any bike that was both efficient and reliable. In most cases the best shot was to go directly to the shop which was an option due to the added hustle of transporting the bike to where I lived.


As such, I decided to come up with a platform through which you could easily rent a bike and have it delivered be it to your home, office, school or just below your hiking trail.

I hope that the platform encourages more people to choose cycling which is both good for your health and for the environment. Cycling is flexible and cheap compared to alternative transport means.



Though this is a passion project, it is also a project for the program run by Holberton School.
